# Shape — Composition Details

**Stage:** Shape  
**Date:** 2025-12-17

---

## Refined Layout (11x17 Portrait)

### Top Third (Visual Focal Point)
- **Champagne toast illustration** (centered, prominent)
  - Two champagne flutes clinking
  - Bubbles rising (dynamic, celebratory)
  - Gold linework with subtle shading
  - City skyline silhouette behind glasses (dark burgundy, subtle)

### Middle Third (Headline & Event Info)
- **"Louie's"** in script font (matching venue logo)
  - Size: Large but secondary to "New Year's Eve"
  - Color: Gold with subtle drop shadow
- **"New Year's Eve Celebration"** in bold art deco sans
  - Size: Dominant headline
  - Color: Gold with slight gradient (lighter at top)
  - Treatment: Letter-spaced for elegance
- **Decorative separator** (thin ornamental line, burgundy)

### Bottom Third (Details & CTA)
- **Event details** in elegant serif (cream color)
  - "December 31, 2025"
  - "9 PM - 2 AM"
  - "123 Main Street, Downtown"
- **Ticket pricing** in bold sans (cream, slightly larger)
  - "$35 Advance | $50 Door"
- **URL** in sans (gold, stands out as CTA)
  - "louies.example.com/nye"
- **Venue logo** (footer, gold, small but clear)

---

## Typography Specifications

**Headline Stack:**
- "Louie's" — Edwardian Script (or similar elegant script)
- "New Year's Eve Celebration" — ITC Anna or Broadway (bold deco sans)

**Body Text:**
- Event details — Didot or Baskerville (elegant serif, 14-16pt)
- Ticket pricing — Futura Bold (sans, 18pt)
- URL — Futura Medium (sans, 16pt)

---

## Color Application

**Background:**
- Deep black (#0A0A0A)
- Subtle paper texture overlay (10% opacity, warm grain)

**Foreground:**
- Gold (#D4AF37) — Headline, champagne illustration, URL, logo
- Cream (#F5F5DC) — Body text, readability
- Burgundy (#6B0F1A) — Border, decorative elements, skyline silhouette

---

## Border & Ornamentation

**Frame:**
- Thin gold border (8pt) around entire poster
- Corner flourishes (art deco curves, not geometric angles)
- Subtle ornamental elements between sections (burgundy, delicate)

**Texture:**
- Vintage paper grain overlay (subtle, doesn't obscure text)
- Slight vignette at edges (draws eye to center)

---

## Instagram Variant (9:16) Adaptations

**Layout changes for social:**
- Champagne illustration scales to fit vertical format
- Headline remains centered, font sizes adjusted proportionally
- Remove corner flourishes (too small to see on mobile)
- Simplify border (thicker line, no ornamental details)
- URL becomes primary CTA (larger, more prominent)

---

## Print Specifications

**Print version (11x17):**
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- Color mode: CMYK (with Pantone gold metallic callout for print shop)
- Bleed: 0.125" on all sides
- Safe area: 0.25" margin from trim
