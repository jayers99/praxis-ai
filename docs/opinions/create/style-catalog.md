# Opinion: Style Catalog

> A collection of reusable visual and aesthetic patterns.

## Core Styles

### Minimalist

- **Triggers:** clean, simple, modern, professional, tech, corporate
- **Palette:** High contrast (Black/White), single accent color
- **Typography:** Sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, Helvetica)
- **Spacing:** Generous white space, strict grid alignment

### Handcrafted

- **Triggers:** organic, warm, personal, artisanal, rustic, holiday
- **Palette:** Earth tones, muted colors, textured backgrounds
- **Typography:** Serif or Script (Playfair Display, Homemade Apple)
- **Spacing:** Relaxed, slightly irregular, layered elements

### Brutalist

- **Triggers:** raw, bold, experimental, urban, high-impact
- **Palette:** Primary colors, neon, clashing combinations
- **Typography:** Monospace or Heavy Display (JetBrains Mono, Impact)
- **Spacing:** Tight, overlapping, breaking the grid

---

## Expressive Dimensions

When defining a style or tone, consider these dimensions:

- **Line:** Bold, delicate, sketchy, clean, fluid, jagged.
- **Palette:** Vibrant, muted, monochromatic, warm, cool, high-contrast.
- **Shape:** Geometric, organic, abstract, representational, distorted.
- **Mood:** Whimsical, nostalgic, elegant, joyful, solemn, mysterious.
- **Texture:** Watercolor, oil paint, charcoal, paper-cut, digital, grainy.
- **Lighting:** Soft glow, dramatic shadows, golden hour, flat, cinematic.
- **Perspective:** Close-up, wide-angle, bird's eye, eye-level, isometric.

---

## Composition Dimensions

When composing the frame, consider these principles:

- **Focal Point:** Center, off-center, rule of thirds, golden ratio.
- **Balance:** Symmetrical, asymmetrical, radial, discordant.
- **Depth:** Layering (foreground, middle ground, background), atmospheric perspective.
- **Negative Space:** Minimalist (high negative space), dense (low negative space), breathing room.
- **Leading Lines:** Diagonal, curved, converging, horizontal/vertical stability.
- **Framing:** Natural frames (trees, windows), architectural, vignettes, internal borders.

---

## Design Constraints

- Maintain consistency within a project or brand
- Establish visual hierarchy through contrast, scale, and spacing
- Choose typography that supports readability and tone
- Use color intentionally to convey meaning and mood

## Requirements

- Define a style guide or reference before execution
- Document color palette, typography, and spacing rules
- Ensure assets are reusable and organized
- Consider context and medium (print, screen, mobile)

## Anti-Patterns

- Mixing too many fonts or colors without purpose
- Inconsistent spacing and alignment
- Ignoring accessibility (contrast, legibility)
- Style choices that fight the content

## Summary

A catalog of intentional, consistent visual choices that serve the content and audience.
