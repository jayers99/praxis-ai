# Example: Praxis Roles in Action

## Scenario
Design and implement a small CLI utility.

### Explore
- Research Librarian gathers background
- Output: research summary

### Shape
- Product Owner frames options
- Red Team challenges assumptions

### Decide
- Synthesis resolves into one direction

### Execute
- Developer builds increment
- Scrum Master enforces scope

### Review
- Synthesis summarizes learnings
- Research updated
