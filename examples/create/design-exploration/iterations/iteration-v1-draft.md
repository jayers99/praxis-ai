## Design Description

**Composition:**
- Champagne toast illustration (two flutes clinking, gold linework) — top third
- City skyline silhouette in burgundy behind champagne glasses (subtle, atmospheric)
- "Louie's" in Edwardian Script (gold, elegant) — centered below illustration
- "New Year's Eve Celebration" in ITC Anna (gold, bold, dominant headline)
- Thin ornamental separator line (burgundy, art deco curves)
- Event details in Didot (cream): date, time, address — middle third
- Ticket pricing in Futura Bold (cream, slightly larger)
- URL in Futura Medium (gold, CTA emphasis)
- Venue logo in footer (gold, small script)
- Art deco border with corner flourishes (gold, 8pt line)
- Vintage paper texture overlay (subtle, warm grain at 10% opacity)

**Color execution:**
- Background: Deep black (#0A0A0A)
- Gold elements: #D4AF37 (champagne, headline, border, logo, URL)
- Burgundy accents: #6B0F1A (skyline, separator, ornamental details)
- Cream text: #F5F5DC (body copy, pricing)
