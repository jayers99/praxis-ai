# Exploration Option 1: Classic Art Deco

**Stage:** Explore  
**Date:** 2025-12-16

---

## Concept

Traditional art deco poster with strong geometric emphasis, heavy use of symmetry and ornamental borders.

---

## Visual Description

**Composition:**
- Strict vertical symmetry
- Heavy geometric border frames the entire poster
- Central sunburst/radiating lines motif
- Champagne bottle as central icon (vertical, aligned with symmetry)

**Typography:**
- Ultra-bold art deco headline (all caps, wide kerning)
- Geometric sans for all supporting text
- Extremely structured, grid-aligned

**Color:**
- Gold on black (high contrast, no burgundy)
- Minimal color variation (stark, dramatic)

**Ornamentation:**
- Corner flourishes (geometric, angular)
- Repeated zigzag patterns (quintessential deco)
- Strong horizontal rules separating sections

---

## Strengths

- ✓ Immediately recognizable as art deco
- ✓ Timeless aesthetic
- ✓ Strong visual hierarchy
- ✓ Print-friendly (clear lines, high contrast)

## Weaknesses

- ✗ May feel too formal/stiff for a party
- ✗ Heavy ornamentation might overwhelm small social media variant
- ✗ Risk of feeling like museum piece, not celebration

---

## Client Feedback (Sketch Review)

"Beautiful, but maybe too traditional? Feels like a gala, not a speakeasy party."

**Decision:** Explore other options before committing
