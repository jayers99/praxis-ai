# Sense — Visual Direction Mood Board

**Stage:** Sense  
**Date:** 2025-12-16

---

## Synthesized Visual Direction

After reviewing all captured references, a clear aesthetic emerges: **Vintage Art Deco NYE Celebration**

---

## Color Palette (Locked In)

**Primary:**
- **Rich Gold (#D4AF37)** — Foil effect, champagne, celebration
- **Deep Black (#0A0A0A)** — Mystery, elegance, contrast

**Accent:**
- **Burgundy (#6B0F1A)** — Warmth, wine, sophistication
- **Cream (#F5F5DC)** — Vintage paper texture, readability

**Application:**
- Background: Black
- Headline: Gold (simulated foil)
- Body text: Cream
- Accents: Burgundy for borders/details

---

## Typography Direction

**Headline (Event Name):**
- Style: Bold art deco geometric
- Inspiration: ITC Anna, Broadway, or custom geometric
- Treatment: Large, centered, gold foil effect

**Details (Date/Time/Location):**
- Style: Elegant serif or refined sans
- Inspiration: Didot or Futura
- Treatment: Smaller, cream color, clean hierarchy

**Call to Action (Ticket Info):**
- Style: Bold sans, high contrast
- Treatment: Prominent but secondary to headline

---

## Visual Motifs

**Central Image:**
- Champagne glasses clinking (toast, celebration)
- OR city skyline silhouette (downtown venue context)
- OR art deco geometric burst (abstract, timeless)

**Framing Elements:**
- Art deco border (geometric corners, ornamental)
- Subtle texture (vintage paper grain)
- Venue logo integrated naturally (top or bottom)

---

## Composition Structure

**Layout:** Vertical (portrait), centered symmetry

**Hierarchy:**
1. Champagne/skyline visual (top third)
2. "Louie's New Year's Eve Celebration" (dominant headline)
3. Art deco ornamental separator
4. Date, time, location (middle third)
5. Ticket pricing and URL (bottom third, call to action)
6. Venue logo (footer or integrated into border)

---

## Tone & Feel

- **Elegant:** Not garish or kitschy, refined vintage
- **Celebratory:** NYE energy, anticipation, glamour
- **Mysterious:** Speakeasy vibe, intimate, exclusive
- **Timeless:** Classic design that won't feel dated next year

---

**Key insight:** The design should feel like a vintage poster from the 1920s that was preserved and rediscovered—authentic art deco, not modern retro pastiche.
